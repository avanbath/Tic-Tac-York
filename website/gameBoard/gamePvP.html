Tips and Tricks:

  • Always place your first symbol in the center or corner! Your first symbol is crucial to winning so putting it in the right spot will give you an edge.

  • Going second may make it tougher to win! Try to capitalize on your opponent's mistakes.

  • Look for moves that can give you a win in 2 ways, that way your opponent cannot block your victory.

How to Play:

  • If you are first, click the blank box you would like to place your symbol in. If you are second, wait for your opponent and then make your move.

  • Both players will continue making moves until a win condition is reached.

  • Win condition: Have 3 of your symbols in a row either vertically, horizontally or diagonally.
